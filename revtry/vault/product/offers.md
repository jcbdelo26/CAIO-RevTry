## Purpose
Defines the product/service offerings that agents reference when crafting outreach. Every campaign draft must reference a specific offer from this file.

## Core Offer
Fractional Chief AI Officer embedding into mid-market firms.

## Offer Stack

| Product | Price | Description | Best For |
|---------|-------|-------------|----------|
| AI Opportunity Audit | $10,000 | Assessment + roadmap — identifies top AI opportunities and builds implementation plan | First engagement; discovery-stage buyers |
| AI Exec Workshop | $12,000 | Day 1 Bootcamp + action plan — intensive executive alignment session | Companies needing team buy-in before committing |
| On-Site Plan | $14,500/mo | Embedded executive — fractional CAIO on-site driving implementation | Companies ready to execute; post-audit |
| Enterprise | Custom | Full-scope engagement — multi-department AI transformation | Large mid-market firms with complex needs |
| Consulting | $800/hr | Ad-hoc advisory — on-demand strategic AI guidance | Existing clients needing spot expertise |

## Bundle Structure
Embedded exec + Day 1 Bootcamp + support layers + risk-free guarantee.

## Primary Mechanism
