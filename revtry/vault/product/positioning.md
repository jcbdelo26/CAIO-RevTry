## Purpose
Defines the target buyer profile, pain points, key messaging, and positioning angles that agents use when crafting outreach copy. Campaign Craft must align every draft with the positioning in this file.

## Target Buyer Profile
- Executives under pressure, skeptical, but open to AI transformation
- Mid-market firms (51-500 employees, $5M-$100M revenue)
- No internal AI leadership — need fractional expertise
- Primary KPI for us: Booked calls with qualified buyers

## Pain Points (use in outreach copy)

| Pain Point | Description | Best Tier Match |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| Stalled AI pilots | Company tried AI, it didn't stick, no one owns it | Tier 1, Tier 2 |
| No AI lead | No CTO/CAIO, leadership knows they need AI but has no one to drive it | Tier 1 |
| Leadership pressure | Board or investors asking about AI strategy, exec has no answer | Tier 1 |
| CTO buried in legacy tech | Technical leader is maintaining existing systems, no bandwidth for AI | Tier 2 |
| Team productivity plateau | Operations team hit efficiency ceiling without automation | Tier 2, Tier 3 |
| Competitor AI adoption | Competitors are using AI, company risks falling behind | Tier 1, Tier 2 |

## Key Messaging Framework

### Primary Promise
"Measurable ROI, or you don't pay the next phase."

### Mechanism
M.A.P. Framework -- Measure, Automate, Prove (90-day cycles)
- **Measure**: Identify highest-ROI AI opportunities in the business
- **Automate**: Build and deploy AI workflows that deliver results
- **Prove**: Quantify impact, iterate, scale what works

### Proof Points

| Metric | Value | Context |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Hours clawed back | 1,000+ | Across client engagements |
| Productivity boost | 27% | Average client improvement |
| Time saved in 30 days | 300+ hours | Rapid deployment results |
| Staff equivalent | 20+ | AI handles work equivalent |
| Cost reduction | 20-30% | Typical engagement outcome |
| Efficiency gain | 40%+ | Process automation results |
| Admin time reduction | 62.5% | Back-office automation |

### Positioning Statement
We are the fractional Chief AI Officer for mid-market companies that know they need AI but don't have the internal leadership to make it happen. We embed, execute, and prove ROI in 90-day cycles.

## Differentiation

| Us | Them |
|----|------|
| Embedded executive (fractional CAIO) | External consultant who leaves a PDF |
| 90-day ROI cycles with guarantee | 6-month projects with no accountability |
| M.A.P. Framework (structured, repeatable) | Ad-hoc AI experiments |
| Measure before automating | Jump to tools without strategy |
